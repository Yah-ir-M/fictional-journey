Santa Monica Pier

The Santa Monica is one of the most popular places in Los Angeles, I would say. From the beach to the actual pier itself, its an overall very fun place to go with your family or friends. There is some places to go to nearby as well to eat and walk around just in case you ever get bored of the pier itself. The pier offers food, rides, games, restaurants, and a very nice view of the Santa Monica Beach where you can watch the waves as they come in. Although the rides are nothing out of this world it is still fun for anyone that decides to go and keeps you occupied enough.
